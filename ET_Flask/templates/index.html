Instructions:
This tool can provide either of the following options for your study:

  • Compute melt pool dimensions
  • Calculate (or download) calculated temperature data
For both cases you will have to provide the following quantities:
  • Heat Source Parameters (applied to L-PBF Additive Manufacturing)
    • Laser Power [W]
    • Scanning Speed [m/s]
    • Laser Beam Size at 4 standard deviations [μm]
  • Material Parameters
    • Absorptivity [dimensionless] (between 0 and 1)
    • Thermal Conductivity [W/(m·K)]
    • Specific Heat Capacity [J/(kg·K)]
    • Density [kg/m3]
    • Melting Temperature [K]
